Gameboy 98 Homepage - Gameboy 98 is a (gasp) Gameboy emulator for Windows 9x. We are both helping eachother out with drawing methods and he's teaching me directdraw/directsound programming, so I feel the best I can do is put a link to him.

uNESsential Homepage - uNESsential is an NES emulator. "NOT ANOTHER ONE," you cry. However this one is different... It's written in 100% QuickBasic. (Yes that's different than QBasic, namely in that it compiles, and you have to pay for it.) I'm helping this guy out with his emulator and he's helping me out with mine. In fact, we both made the same mistake in our cpu cores, but he found the bug and told me about it, and we both went on to our respective emulators to continue our tireless work.

Shyguy's Nintendo Emulation Page - An interesting ROMs page.

Archaic Ruins - Probably the oldest emulation site on the net that's still running strong. The guy who's currently maintaining it (not Chris, haven't talked to him yet) is pretty cool. While it's not a very well known site, it's probably the site with the most detail about all emulators while at the same time a great source for emulator info. The news page is also very detailed and well-kept!

Zophars Domain - The 2nd best news site on the 'net! Has more information than Emu News service, is updated more and with more info, and is run by a guy who actually goes on IRC. =)
