NES4PC Info:

NES4PC is an NES emulator for Windows 95 with DirectX v5.0+, Windows 98, and Windows NT with Service Pack 3 or higher.

Features:

  • All 6502 Opcodes. All registers are either fully supported or have code in to make them work until I add full functionality to them.
  • FULL PPU Emulation with all types of mirroring including 4-screen, all sprite functions including tile flipping, and a pretty good palette.
  • DirectSound Sound engine with Pulse 1/2 currently emulated!
  • Scanline-based graphics engine!
  • 2 Joypads, full paddle emulation, and zapper emulation
  • An accurate & speedy 8-bit mode, and slow 16-bit, 24-bit, and 32-bit color modes.
  • Automatic frameskipping
